About Us

Bio Care Consumers was initiated by Mr.P.Y.Amaralal Dias in 2009 as a Sole Proprietorship, and legally registered in Panadura, Western Province, Sri Lanka.
Bio Care Consumers pioneered the production of a beverage that included Aloe Vera jelly quantities and this is being marketed since 2010 in Colombo. There is an enthusiastic demand for the beverage that is steadily growing and now has the potential of being expanded island wide. The induction of the beverage into the markets started on a very small scale. However, due to the heavy increase in demand, 2011 saw the investment by the company in new machinery and equipment to cater to the increased demand.
Presently, the company is manufacturing over 100,000 bottles a month. The processing facility is maintained under heavy scrutiny. Highest quality assurance is focused upon to assured a product of premier quality and zero tolerance of non-conforming products.
